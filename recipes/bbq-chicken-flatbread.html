BBQ Chicken Flatbread

Description

With a few household staples, you can serve up this BBQ chicken flatbread that tastes like a food truck special.

With sweet and tangy BBQ sauce and the bite from banana peppers, this easy weeknight meal packs a perfect punch.

Ingredients

  • 1 round naan
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce of choice
  • 2-3 cooked chicken fingers
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions (pickled red onions are great too!)
  • Pickled banana peppers
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese of choice
  • Cilantro for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees farenheit.
  2. Place naan on baking sheet.
  3. Use spoon to spread BBQ evenly across naan.
  4. Top with chopped chicken fingers, onions, and banana peppers.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  6. Bake for approx. 11-12 minutes or until cheese is melted and begins to brown.
  7. Slice and top with chopped cilantro.

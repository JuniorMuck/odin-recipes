Grilled Cheese

Description

The classic sandwich, this recipe will leave you coming back for more.

A simple recipe with a scrumptious result. Melty, gooey cheddar wedged between two thick slices of perfectly toasted Italian bread, ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Italian bread, cut thick
  • 2 slices of aged sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp. mayonaisse

Steps

  1. Place the slices of cheddar cheese atop one slice of Italian bread.
  2. Place the second slice of Italian bread on top to close the sandwiwch.
  3. Generously spread mayonaisse on the outside of both the top and bottom slice of bread.
  4. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Grill sandwich for 4 minutes, or until bread is golden brown.
  6. Flip your sandwich and grill for an additional 4 minutes or until both sides are golden brown and the sliced cheese has melted.
  7. Plate your sandwich and slice diagonally. Let cool for 1-2 minutes.
  8. Dig in and be transported to flavour heaven.

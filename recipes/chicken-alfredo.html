Chicken Alfredo

Description

This simplified version of the decadent classic makes a perfect (and quick!) weeknight dinner.

Pair this recipe with a loaf of garlic bread and bottle of red wine for the perfect weeknight treat.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. of chicken breast or thighs
  • 1/2 tbsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp. oil of choice (oil from sundried tomatoes adds a great flavour!)
  • 1lb. of your favourite dried pasta
  • 1 jar of alfredo sauce
  • 3 cloves of fresh garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Chopped parsley (optional)
  • Grated parmesean cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice your chicken breast in 1/2 inch thick slices and season generously with paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in shallow pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Fry chicken for approx. 4 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  4. While the chicken is cooking, fill a large pot with cold water. Add a generous heaping of salt and put on stove to boil.
  5. When your water reaches a boil, add pasta and cook until al dente.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked through, add minced garlic to pan and stir until fragrant.
  7. Stir in jar of alfredo sauce and bring to a simmer over medium-low heat.
  8. Allow sauce to simmer until pasta is cooked. Season to taste with salt and freshly cracked black pepper.
  9. Reserve 1/8 cup of pasta water and drain pasta.
  10. Add reserved pasta water to sauce and stir to combine.
  11. Combine cooked noodles and sauce. Toss to combine.
  12. Top with chopped parsley and grated parmesean.
